## 01. The depth scale

Five neutral tokens form a progression from subtle to dramatic. Each step
increases the vertical offset, blur radius, and opacity, simulating an element
lifting further from the surface beneath it. The scale gives every component
a precise amount of perceived distance.

## 02. The glow

A glow is a drop-shadow with a brand color instead of black. That single change
transforms its purpose: where a neutral shadow communicates distance, a colored
glow communicates <strong>importance</strong>. Apply it to a raised card and
the card becomes a highlighted card, amplifying its visual gravity without
changing its content or layout.

## 03. Static vs. motion

Neutral shadows are always static. They are infrastructure: a dropdown's shadow
should never compete with the dropdown's content. Glows, however, can be
animated to attract even more attention.

<div className="flex flex-col mt-6" style={{ gap: '24px' }}>

<div className="rounded-xl border border-border bg-card p-6">
  <span className="font-semibold text-foreground block" style={{ fontSize: '1.5rem' }}>Static glow</span>
  <span className="uppercase block mb-3" style={{ fontSize: '12px', fontWeight: 600, letterSpacing: '0.1em', color: 'var(--muted-foreground)', marginTop: '8px' }}>Quiet promotion</span>
  <p className="text-sm text-muted-foreground m-0" style={{ lineHeight: 1.6 }}>
    A colored <code className="text-xs bg-muted px-1 py-0.5 rounded">box-shadow</code> applied
    directly to the element. It highlights without movement, suitable when
    promotion should be present but not distracting.
  </p>
</div>

<div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-brand-accent-200 dark:border-brand-accent-800 bg-card p-6">
  <span className="font-semibold text-foreground block" style={{ fontSize: '1.5rem' }}>Animated glow</span>
  <span className="uppercase block mb-3" style={{ fontSize: '12px', fontWeight: 600, letterSpacing: '0.1em', color: 'var(--muted-foreground)', marginTop: '8px' }}>Active promotion</span>
  <p className="text-sm text-muted-foreground m-0" style={{ lineHeight: 1.6 }}>
    The <code className="text-xs bg-muted px-1 py-0.5 rounded">glow-standout</code> utility
    rotates a conic gradient (accent-300 to primary-700) through two
    counter-rotating blur layers. Use it when a single element needs to be the
    undeniable focal point: a KPI card, a search bar, a call-to-action. One per
    viewport at most. Respects
    <code className="text-xs bg-muted px-1 py-0.5 rounded">prefers-reduced-motion</code> by
    freezing the gradient at 135 degrees.
  </p>
</div>
