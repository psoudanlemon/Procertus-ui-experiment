## The "Midnight" neutral

A platform built entirely on Navy and Teal would be visually overwhelming.
The bulk of the interface, body text, backgrounds, and borders, lives in
neutral tones to keep the experience calm and readable. But we don't
reach for pure black and pure white. Although they offer strong contrast,
they make every platform look the same. Instead, we infuse a hint of
brand navy into every neutral (hue ~230), creating our "Midnight" shades
that feel more refined and premium.

## The contrast contract

Accessibility is not a checklist. It is the orchestration layer of our
color system: if a combination does not meet the contrast contract, it
does not enter the theme. A **contract of inclusion** that ensures every
surface is legible for every user.
